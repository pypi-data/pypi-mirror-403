# Define Platform-Wide AI Boundaries for an Organization

Objective:

- Define where AI is allowed and not allowed across the platform.
- Establish hard organizational boundaries rather than system-specific rules.
- Issue a decision that constrains future AI usage.

---
Requirements:

- You must edit and replace the contents of `platform_boundary_review.md`
  under the provided headings.
- All placeholder guidance must be removed before validation.

---
Task Requirements:

- Assess platform areas where AI introduces unacceptable risk.
- Identify domains where AI use must be categorically disallowed.
- Define areas where AI may be permitted with human ownership.
- Justify boundaries under executive and regulatory scrutiny.

---
Constraints:

- No tooling, implementation, or enforcement mechanisms.
- No mitigation, safeguards, or approval workflows.
- No system-level evaluation.
- No conditional or temporary boundaries.
- Offline, judgment-based decision only.
