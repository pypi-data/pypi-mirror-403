# Define Platform-Wide AI Boundaries for Human Ownership and Responsibility

Objective:

- Define where AI is allowed and disallowed based on human ownership.
- Treat ownership as a non-transferable responsibility.
- Issue boundaries that ensure accountability always rests with people.

Requirements:

- You must edit and replace the contents of `human_ownership_boundary_review.md`
  under the provided headings.

Constraints:
