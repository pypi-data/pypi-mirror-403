# AI involvement in incident resolution actions

Objective:

Define explicit boundaries for AI involvement in incident resolution actions.

---
Requirements:

- You are responsible for resolving production incidents.
- A proposal suggests allowing AI to assist with resolution actions.
- **You must edit and replace the contents of `resolution-boundary.md` under the provided headings.**

---
Task Requirements:

- Define where AI may assist during incident resolution.
- Define where AI must stop and human control is mandatory.
- Record these boundaries clearly and unambiguously.

---
Constraints:

- You may not assume resolution actions are reversible.
- You may not rely on post-action review as a safeguard.
- You may not design automation or execution workflows.
- This is a boundary definition task, not an execution exercise.
