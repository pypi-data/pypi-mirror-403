# Incident Escalation and Authority

Incident escalation transfers responsibility
from one individual or team to another.

## Why Escalation Is Sensitive
- It assigns authority and accountability
- It interrupts people and teams
- It signals severity to the organization

## Why Teams Consider AI Assistance
- Faster recognition of serious incidents
- Reduced hesitation during escalation
- Support for less-experienced responders

## Known Operational Risks
- Failure to escalate when required
- Over-escalation causing fatigue
- Unclear ownership of escalation decisions
- AI influence masking human accountability

This document provides context only.
It does not define acceptable escalation boundaries.
