# AI Must Stop in Change Approval

Objective:

Define explicit boundaries for AI involvement in production change approval.

---
Requirements:

- You are responsible for a production change management process.
- A proposal suggests using AI to assist with approving changes.
- **You must edit and replace the contents of `change-approval-boundary.md` under the provided headings.**

---
Task Requirements:

- Define where AI may assist in the change approval process.
- Define where AI must stop and human authority is mandatory.
- Record these boundaries clearly and unambiguously.

---
Constraints:

- You may not assume approved changes are reversible.
- You may not rely on post-change review as a safeguard.
- You may not design approval systems or workflows.
- This is a boundary definition task, not a governance implementation exercise.
