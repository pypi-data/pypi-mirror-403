# Decide Removal of an AIOps System After Cumulative Low-Severity Harm

Objective:

- Decide whether a live AIOps system must be permanently removed
  due to cumulative low-severity harm.
- Evaluate aggregate impact rather than isolated incidents.
- Issue a decision that remains valid after long-term review.

---
Requirements:

- You must edit and replace the contents of `cumulative_harm_review.md`
  under the provided headings.
- All placeholder guidance must be removed before validation.

---
Task Requirements:

- Assess recurring low-impact failures linked to the system.
- Evaluate how cumulative harm affects organizational reliability.
- Identify who owns the risk of continued exposure.
- Decide whether the system should be removed or retained.
- Justify the decision under executive and audit scrutiny.

---
Constraints:

- No mitigation, remediation, or tuning proposals.
- No reliance on severity thresholds or tolerance models.
- No conditional or reversible decisions.
- No tooling, metrics, or model discussion.
- Offline, judgment-based decision only.
