# Decide Removal of an AIOps System After Accountability Collapse

Objective:

- Decide whether a live AIOps system must be permanently removed
  after accountability for its decisions has collapsed.
- Treat accountability as a governance prerequisite.
- Issue a decision that remains valid after organizational failure.

---
Requirements:

- You must edit and replace the contents of `accountability_collapse_review.md`
  under the provided headings.
- All placeholder guidance must be removed before validation.

---
Task Requirements:

- Assess how responsibility for outcomes has become unclear.
- Evaluate organizational impact of diffused ownership.
- Identify who implicitly carries risk if the system remains active.
- Decide whether the system should be removed or retained.
- Justify the decision for executive and audit review.

---
Constraints:

- No mitigation, remediation, or role redesign proposals.
- No governance process additions or clarifications.
- No conditional or reversible decisions.
- No tooling, metrics, or model discussion.
- Offline, judgment-based decision only.
