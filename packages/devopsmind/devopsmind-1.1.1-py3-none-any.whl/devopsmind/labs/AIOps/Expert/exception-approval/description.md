# Decide Whether to Grant an Exception for AIOps System Approval

Objective:

- Evaluate whether an exception-based approval should be granted
  for an AIOps system that does not meet standard criteria.
- Assess governance and risk implications of granting exceptions.
- Make a clear approval or denial decision.

---
Requirements:

- You must edit and replace the contents of `exception_approval_review.md`
  under the provided headings.
- All instructional scaffolding must be removed before validation.

---
Task Requirements:

- Describe why the system does not meet standard approval criteria.
- Identify the reasons an exception is being requested.
- Evaluate the risks introduced by granting an exception.
- Assess accountability if an exception-approved system fails.
- Decide whether the exception should be granted or denied.

---
Constraints:

- No conditional or temporary exceptions.
- No compensating controls or safeguards.
- No system redesign or remediation.
- Offline, judgment-based decision only.
- Do not assign blame to individuals.
