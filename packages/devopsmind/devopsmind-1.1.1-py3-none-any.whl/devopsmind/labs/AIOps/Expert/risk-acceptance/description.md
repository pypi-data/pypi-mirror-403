# Decide Organizational Risk Acceptance for an AIOps System

Objective:

- Evaluate whether the organization should formally accept
  the risks introduced by an AIOps system.
- Assess the consequences of accepting unresolved risk.
- Make a clear risk acceptance or rejection decision.

---
Requirements:

- You must edit and replace the contents of `risk_acceptance_review.md`
  under the provided headings.
- All instructional scaffolding must be removed before validation.

---
Task Requirements:

- Describe the AI system and its known risks.
- Evaluate the severity and likelihood of those risks.
- Assess who bears responsibility if risks materialize.
- Decide whether the organization should accept or reject the risk.
- Justify the decision under potential future incident review.

---
Constraints:

- No mitigation or remediation proposals.
- No conditional acceptance.
- No additional controls or safeguards.
- Offline, judgment-based decision only.
- Do not assign blame to individuals.
