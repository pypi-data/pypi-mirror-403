# Enterprise AIOps Governance Review

## Decision
State the governance decision clearly.

---

## Scope
Define where the AIOps system is permitted to operate
and where it is explicitly restricted.

---

## Risk Assessment
Identify material risks introduced by the system.
Focus on operational, organizational, and failure-mode risk.

---

## Accountability
Define ownership and responsibility.
Accountability must be enforceable, not aspirational.

---

## Audit & Traceability
Describe how decisions and influence can be reconstructed
after incidents or failures.

---

## Conditions
If approval is conditional, list enforceable conditions.
If rejected, state what prevents approval.
