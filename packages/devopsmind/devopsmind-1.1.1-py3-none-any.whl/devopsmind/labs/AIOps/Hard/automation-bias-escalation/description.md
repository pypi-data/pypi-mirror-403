# Assess Impact of Automation Bias on Incident Escalation

Objective:

- Analyze the operational risk created when automation bias delays
  escalation during a production incident.
- Evaluate how AI guidance influenced human hesitation or inaction.
- Determine accountability and post-incident decisions.

---
Requirements:

- You must edit and replace the contents of `automation_bias_assessment.md` under the provided headings.
- All instructional scaffolding must be removed before validation.

---
Task Requirements:

- Describe the incident and escalation timeline.
- Analyze how AI guidance influenced escalation decisions.
- Identify signals that contradicted the automated recommendation.
- Assess the operational and business impact of delayed escalation.
- Decide what action must be taken regarding AI involvement in escalation.

---
Constraints:

- No AI retraining or tuning.
- No changes to escalation tooling or policies.
- No new alerts or observability mechanisms.
- Offline, judgment-based analysis only.
- Do not assign blame to individuals.
