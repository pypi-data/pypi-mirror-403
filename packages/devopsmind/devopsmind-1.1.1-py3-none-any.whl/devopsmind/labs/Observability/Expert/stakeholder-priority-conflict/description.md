# Resolve stakeholder priority conflicts during an incident

Objective:

- Practice decision-making when engineering, business, and compliance priorities conflict.
- Balance system stability, customer impact, and organizational risk.
- Communicate a defensible decision under pressure.

---
Requirements:

- Notes from multiple stakeholders are provided.
- Alerts and metrics describe an ongoing incident.
- The system state is preloaded to simulate a live production environment.
- The task must be completed fully offline.

---
Task Requirements:

- Review stakeholder notes, alerts, and metrics.
- Identify the competing priorities and constraints.
- Decide on a course of action that balances risk and impact.
- Clearly justify your decision.
- Record your decision in **stakeholder-decision.md**.

---
Constraints:

- Do NOT run commands or scripts.
- Do NOT attempt permanent fixes.
- Do NOT ignore any stakeholder perspective.
- Focus on decision quality and justification, not consensus.
