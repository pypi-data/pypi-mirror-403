# Own risk for an irreversible incident decision

Objective:

- Practice deciding whether to take an irreversible action during an incident.
- Evaluate long-term risk versus short-term impact reduction.
- Demonstrate ownership of decision consequences.

---
Requirements:

- The system is experiencing ongoing degradation.
- A proposed action would reduce impact but is difficult to reverse.
- Observability data is incomplete.
- The system state is preloaded.
- The task must be completed fully offline.

---
Task Requirements:

- Review the provided context, alerts, and metrics.
- Evaluate the proposed irreversible action.
- Decide whether to proceed or refuse the action.
- Explicitly own the risks of your decision.
- Record your decision in **risk-ownership.md**.

---
Constraints:

- Do NOT run commands or scripts.
- Do NOT propose alternative reversible fixes.
- Do NOT avoid making a decision.
- Focus on accountability and risk ownership.
