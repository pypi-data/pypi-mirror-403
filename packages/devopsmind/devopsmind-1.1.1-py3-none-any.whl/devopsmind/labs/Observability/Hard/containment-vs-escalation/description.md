# Decide between containment and escalation during an incident

Objective:

- Practice deciding whether an incident should be contained locally or escalated.
- Learn to assess risk, uncertainty, and blast radius.
- Communicate escalation decisions clearly and responsibly.

---
Requirements:

- Alerts and metrics from an active incident are provided.
- Observability data is incomplete.
- The system state is preloaded to simulate a live incident.
- The task must be completed fully offline.

---
Task Requirements:

- Review the provided alerts and metrics.
- Decide whether the situation requires escalation or continued containment.
- Justify your decision using available evidence.
- Explicitly state what risks influenced your decision.
- Record your decision in **decision-log.md**.

---
Constraints:

- Do NOT run commands or scripts.
- Do NOT attempt to fix the root cause.
- Do NOT assume certainty where evidence is incomplete.
- Do NOT escalate without justification.
- Focus on decision quality, not outcomes.
