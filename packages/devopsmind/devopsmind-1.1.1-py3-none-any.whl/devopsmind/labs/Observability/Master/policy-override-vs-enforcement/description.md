# Decide between policy enforcement and override during an incident

Objective:

- Practice governance-level decision-making under extreme pressure.
- Evaluate when policy enforcement protects the organization and when
  a temporary override may be justified.
- Demonstrate accountability for policy-related risk.

---
Requirements:

- A high-impact production incident is ongoing.
- Organizational policies and stakeholder requests are provided.
- The system state is preloaded to simulate real incident pressure.
- The task must be completed fully offline.

---
Task Requirements:

- Review the policy, incident context, and stakeholder requests.
- Decide whether to enforce policy or approve a temporary override.
- Clearly state the scope, duration, and safeguards of your decision.
- Explicitly own the organizational risk.
- Record your decision in **policy-decision.md**.

---
Constraints:

- Do NOT run commands or scripts.
- Do NOT ignore policy without justification.
- Do NOT approve unlimited or undefined overrides.
- Focus on governance, accountability, and trust.
