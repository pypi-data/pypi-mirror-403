# Make an incident command governance decision

Objective:

- Practice acting as Incident Commander during a high-impact outage.
- Apply governance, policy, and leadership judgment under pressure.
- Make decisions that balance speed, safety, and organizational trust.

---
Requirements:

- A major production incident is in progress.
- Organizational policies and incident updates are provided.
- The system state is preloaded to simulate a live incident.
- The task must be completed fully offline.

---
Task Requirements:

- Review the incident timeline, status updates, and governance policies.
- Assume the role of Incident Commander.
- Decide how the incident should be managed at the organizational level.
- Address communication, authority, and guardrails.
- Record your decisions in **command-decision.md**.

---
Constraints:

- Do NOT run commands or scripts.
- Do NOT propose technical fixes.
- Do NOT ignore policy or governance constraints.
- Focus on leadership decisions, not implementation.
