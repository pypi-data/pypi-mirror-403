# Evaluate alerts against service SLOs

Objective:

- Learn to evaluate alerts in the context of service reliability goals.
- Understand that not all alerts threaten user-facing reliability.
- Practice prioritizing work based on SLO impact.

---
Requirements:

- A set of alerts and a service SLO definition are provided.
- Signals represent the same service and time window.
- The task must be completed fully offline.

---
Task Requirements:

- Review the provided alerts.
- Review the service SLO definition.
- Determine whether the alerts indicate a risk to the SLO.
- Explain your reasoning.
- Record your assessment in **slo-impact-assessment.md**.

---
Constraints:

- Do NOT run commands or scripts.
- Do NOT modify any provided files.
- Do NOT redesign alerts or SLOs.
- Do NOT perform root cause analysis.
- Focus on reliability impact, not fixes.
