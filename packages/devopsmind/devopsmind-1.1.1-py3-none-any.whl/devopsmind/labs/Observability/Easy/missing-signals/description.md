# Identify missing or incomplete signals

Objective:

- Learn to recognize gaps in observability data.
- Understand how missing signals limit incident understanding.
- Practice identifying what information is unavailable or insufficient.

---
Requirements:

- A partial set of application logs is provided.
- Logs represent a time window during a user-reported issue.
- The task must be completed fully offline.

---
Task Requirements:

- Review the provided log data.
- Identify what information is present.
- Identify what important signals are missing or incomplete.
- Record your analysis in **signal-gaps.md**.

---
Constraints:

- Do NOT run commands or scripts.
- Do NOT modify the log file.
- Do NOT invent data that is not present.
- Do NOT attempt to diagnose a root cause.
- Focus only on visibility gaps and limitations.
