# Email Acknowledgment Phrases

## Quick Acknowledgments
- "Thank you for bringing this to our attention."
- "We appreciate you taking the time to contact us."
- "Thank you for your patience."
- "We understand your concern."
- "We value your feedback."

## Apology Phrases
- "We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused."
- "We apologize for the delay in responding."
- "We regret any frustration this situation may have caused."
- "Please accept our apologies for the oversight."

## Positive Responses
- "We're delighted to help."
- "We're happy to assist with this."
- "We're pleased to provide this information."
- "We're glad we could resolve this for you."

## Setting Expectations
- "We're currently investigating this matter."
- "We'll get back to you within [timeframe]."
- "Our team is working on this and will update you shortly."
- "We're prioritizing this and will provide an update soon."
