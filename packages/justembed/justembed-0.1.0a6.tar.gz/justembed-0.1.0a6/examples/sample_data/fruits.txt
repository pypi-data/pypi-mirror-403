Apples are one of the most popular fruits worldwide. They come in many varieties, with colors ranging from bright red to golden yellow and even green. Red apples like Red Delicious and Fuji are particularly sweet and crisp.

Strawberries are small, red berries that are loved for their sweet and slightly tart flavor. They are rich in vitamin C and antioxidants. Fresh strawberries are perfect for desserts, smoothies, or eating on their own.

Cherries are small, round fruits that can be sweet or tart. Sweet cherries are typically dark red or almost black when ripe, while sour cherries are bright red. They are often used in pies, jams, and as toppings for desserts.

Watermelons are large, refreshing fruits with a green rind and red or pink flesh. They are perfect for hot summer days and contain mostly water, making them very hydrating. The sweet, juicy flesh is a favorite at picnics and barbecues.

Tomatoes, while technically a fruit, are often used as vegetables in cooking. They come in various colors including red, yellow, and orange. Red tomatoes are the most common and are used in sauces, salads, and sandwiches.
